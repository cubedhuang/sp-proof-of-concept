about the association

kulupu pali li seme

The sitelen pona Publishers and Typographers Association represents the collective interests of publishers and fontmakers who use the sitelen pona writing system for the toki pona language. Drawing members from around the globe, we engage in content creation, standard setting, advocacy, networking, and collaboration to ensure sitelen pona continues to thrive.
